Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Find My Plugin collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. If something isn't clear, email us at hello@findmyplugin.example.

What we collect

We collect only the information required to operate the site and provide its features:

  • Account data: name, email, and password (passwords are stored hashed).
  • Authentication cookie: a secure HTTP-only cookie used to authenticate requests to the backend.
  • Contact & request forms: name, email, phone (optional), and message content submitted via forms.
  • Local browser data: non-sensitive demo data (e.g. plugin requests) may be stored in localStorage when the backend is unavailable.

How we use data

We use collected data to:

  • Authenticate you and manage your session.
  • Respond to contact messages and plugin requests.
  • Provide and improve site features and functionality.

Cookies & session behavior

We use an authentication cookie (HTTP-only) to identify you on the server. By default the server issues a session cookie (no Expires/Max-Age) so the browser removes it when you close it. If the cookie is configured with an expiry on the server it may persist across restarts — see your account logout options to end a session.

Security & retention

Passwords are stored hashed; server endpoints validate authentication on every request. We retain contact messages and account data as required to provide the service. If you want your data deleted, contact us at hello@findmyplugin.example.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with third-party providers for hosting, email delivery, or analytics under contractual obligations to protect your data.

Your choices

  • Sign out: click Logout to end your session. Closing the browser will also remove session cookies when the server issues session-only cookies.
  • Delete data: email us to request deletion of your account or data.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email: hello@findmyplugin.example.
